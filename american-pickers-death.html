The History of American Pickers

The show follows antique and collectible pickers Mike Wolfe and Frank Fritz as they travel across the United States in search of hidden treasures in people’s homes, barns, and warehouses. The program showcases not only the items they discover but also the stories behind them, highlighting the rich history and nostalgia associated with American culture.

Throughout its run, American Pickers has garnered a loyal fanbase, due in part to its unique premise and the charismatic personalities of its hosts.
