History of American Pickers

American Pickers first premiered on the History Channel on January 18, 2010. Created by Mike Wolfe, the show was born out of his lifelong passion for picking and collecting antiques. Wolfe's vision was to highlight the stories behind the items and the people who owned them, providing a unique perspective on American culture.

Over the years, American Pickers has gained a loyal following, becoming one of the top-rated shows on the History Channel. With its engaging format and relatable hosts, the show has not only entertained viewers but has also educated them about the value of history and the importance of preserving it.

Key Milestones

  • 2010: Premiered on the History Channel
  • 2011: The show became a top-rated program on the network
  • 2019: Celebrated its 10th anniversary with special episodes

Meet the Hosts

American Pickers features two primary hosts, Mike Wolfe and Frank Fritz. Their contrasting personalities and complementary skills make for an entertaining viewing experience.

Mike Wolfe

Mike Wolfe is the creator and lead host of American Pickers. With a passion for antiques that began in his childhood, he has developed a keen eye for valuable items. Wolfe's warm demeanor and storytelling ability make him a relatable figure for audiences.

Frank Fritz

Frank Fritz, co-host of the show, is known for his expertise in vintage items and his enthusiasm for the hunt. With a background in restoration and a love for history, Fritz brings a unique perspective to the picking process.

Format of the Show

The format of American Pickers is straightforward yet captivating. Each episode typically involves the hosts traveling to various locations, meeting with collectors, and searching for hidden treasures. The excitement builds as they negotiate prices and uncover the history behind each item.

Typical Episode Structure

  • Introduction of the location and its owner
  • Exploration of the property
  • Negotiation and purchase of items
  • Conclusion and recap of the finds

Cultural Impact of the Show

American Pickers has not only entertained audiences but has also contributed to the resurgence of interest in antiques and collectibles. The show's focus on history and storytelling has inspired many viewers to explore their own family heirlooms and forgotten treasures.
