Biography of Cillian Murphy

Cillian Murphy was born on May 25, 1976, in Douglas, County Cork, Ireland. He grew up in a family with a strong appreciation for the arts, which influenced his eventual career in acting. Murphy's journey in the entertainment industry began in the late 1990s, and he quickly gained recognition for his roles in films such as "28 Days Later," "Inception," and "Peaky Blinders."
