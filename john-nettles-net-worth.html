1. Biography of John Nettles

John Nettles is a well-known British actor and author, born on 11th October 1943 in St. Austell, Cornwall, England. He gained widespread fame for his portrayal of DCI Tom Barnaby in the hit television series "Midsomer Murders," which aired from 1997 to 2011. Nettles has also appeared in various other television programs and films, showcasing his versatility as an actor.
