Biography of Frank Fritz

Frank Fritz was born on October 11, 1965, in Davenport, Iowa. From a young age, he developed a keen interest in antiques, leading to a lifelong passion for collecting unique items. He co-founded the Antique Archeology shop in Le Claire, Iowa, which became the base for many of his adventures on "American Pickers."
