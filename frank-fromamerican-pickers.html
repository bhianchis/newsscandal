Biography of Frank Fritz

Frank Fritz was born on October 11, 1965, in Davenport, Iowa. His passion for antiques began at a young age, influenced by his family’s interest in collectibles. Over the years, Frank has managed to carve a niche for himself in the world of antique collecting, becoming a prominent figure through his appearances on television.

Career in Antique Collecting

Before his television fame, Frank worked in various jobs, including a firefighter and a landscape designer. However, his true passion lay in collecting antiques. He began his journey by buying and selling items, which eventually turned into a full-time career.

Development of Expertise

Over the years, Frank developed an extensive knowledge of antiques, focusing on American history and the significance of various collectibles. His expertise has been instrumental in helping him identify valuable items during his picking adventures.

American Pickers: The Breakthrough Show

In 2010, Frank Fritz, alongside Mike Wolfe, co-founded the reality television show American Pickers. The show quickly gained popularity, showcasing the duo's adventures as they traveled across the United States in search of hidden treasures.

American Pickers became a cultural phenomenon, attracting millions of viewers and establishing Frank and Mike as household names. The show not only entertained audiences but also educated them about the world of antiques and the significance of preserving history.
