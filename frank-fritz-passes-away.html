Early Life and Background

Frank Fritz was born and raised in Davenport, Iowa. From an early age, he developed a passion for collecting and restoring antiques. His fascination with history and vintage items was evident during his childhood, where he would often explore flea markets and garage sales with his family.

Through hard work and determination, Frank honed his skills in identifying valuable collectibles. After graduating from high school, he pursued various jobs, including working as a fireman, before fully committing to his passion for antiques.

Career Highlights

Frank's big break came when he was approached to co-host "American Pickers" alongside Mike Wolfe. The show, which premiered on the History Channel in 2010, quickly gained a massive following due to its unique premise of exploring America’s hidden treasures.

Throughout its run, "American Pickers" featured Frank and Mike traveling across the country, uncovering forgotten artifacts and sharing their stories. The show's success not only showcased Frank's expertise but also his infectious enthusiasm for finding treasures.
