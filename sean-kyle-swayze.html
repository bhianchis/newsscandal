Biography of Sean Kyle Swayze

Sean Kyle Swayze was born on October 2, 1982, in Houston, Texas. He is the son of the late actor Patrick Swayze and Lisa Niemi, a talented actress and dancer. His father's illustrious career in films such as "Dirty Dancing" and "Ghost" set a high bar for him to aspire to.
