2. Biography of Air Astana

Founded in 2001, Air Astana is the national airline of Kazakhstan, headquartered in Almaty. The airline operates domestic and international flights to over 60 destinations. Air Astana has received multiple awards for its service quality, safety, and operational efficiency, making it a reputable choice for travelers in Central Asia.
