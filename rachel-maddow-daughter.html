Biography of Rachel Maddow

Rachel Anne Maddow was born on April 1, 1973, in Castro Valley, California. She attended Stanford University, where she earned her degree in political science, and later received her Doctor of Philosophy (DPhil) in politics from Oxford University. Her career in broadcasting began in the early 2000s, and she quickly rose to prominence as a host of "The Rachel Maddow Show" on MSNBC.
