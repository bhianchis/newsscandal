Biography of Frank Fritz

Frank Fritz was born on October 11, 1965, in Davenport, Iowa. He developed an interest in antiques and collectibles from a young age, which eventually led him to co-found the antique and collectible store, Frank Fritz Finds. His passion for treasure hunting caught the attention of producers, leading to his role in the hit television series American Pickers, which premiered on the History Channel in 2010.
