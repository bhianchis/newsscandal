1. Biography of Frank Fritz

Frank Fritz was born on October 11, 1965, in Davenport, Iowa. From a young age, he displayed a keen interest in collecting antiques and unique items. His passion led him to explore flea markets, garage sales, and auctions, where he honed his skills in identifying valuable treasures.

Early Influences

Growing up, Frank was influenced by his family, who often encouraged his collecting habits. He developed a strong sense of value and history surrounding objects, which later became a hallmark of his career.

Rise to Fame

Frank's rise to fame began when he co-hosted American Pickers alongside Mike Wolfe in 2010. The show quickly gained popularity, showcasing their adventures as they traveled across the country to find hidden gems in people's homes, barns, and antique shops.
