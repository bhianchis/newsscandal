Career Milestones of the Backstreet Boys

The Backstreet Boys have had a remarkable career filled with numerous milestones. From their formation to their first hit single, "I Want It That Way," their journey has been nothing short of extraordinary. Here are some of the key milestones in their career:

  • 1993: The Backstreet Boys were formed in Orlando, Florida.
  • 1996: They released their self-titled debut album, which included hits like "We've Got It Goin' On."
  • 1999: The release of their third album, "Millennium," catapulted them to international fame with hits such as "I Want It That Way."
  • 2000: The group won their first American Music Award for Favorite Pop/Rock Band.
  • 2007: They celebrated their 14th anniversary with a new album, "Unbreakable."
  • 2019: The Backstreet Boys were honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
