Biography of Frank Fritz

Frank Fritz, born on October 11, 1965, in Davenport, Iowa, was a well-known antique collector and television personality. He gained fame as the co-host of "American Pickers," a reality television series that aired on the History Channel, focusing on the adventure of picking antiques across America.

Early Life and Interests

From a young age, Frank showed a keen interest in collecting antiques. Raised in a family that appreciated history, he began his journey by exploring flea markets and garage sales, which eventually led him to turn his passion into a career.

Rise to Fame

Frank's big break came when he was cast in "American Pickers," alongside Mike Wolfe. The show became a huge success, showcasing their adventures as they traveled across the country in search of unique collectibles.
