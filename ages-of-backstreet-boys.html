Biography of the Backstreet Boys

The Backstreet Boys are an American boy band formed in 1993, consisting of members AJ McLean, Howie Dorough, Nick Carter, Kevin Richardson, and Brian Littrell. They achieved immense popularity in the late 1990s and early 2000s, becoming one of the best-selling music artists of all time. With their catchy songs and impressive vocal harmonies, they have left an indelible mark on the music industry.
