Biography of Frank Fritz

Frank Fritz was born on October 11, 1965, in Davenport, Iowa. From a young age, he developed a keen interest in collecting items that others considered junk. This passion led him to explore flea markets, garage sales, and estate auctions, where he honed his skills in identifying valuable antiques and collectibles.

Frank’s early life was filled with adventures as he explored the backroads of Iowa, searching for hidden treasures. His innate ability to spot valuable items among the clutter set him apart from other collectors. After years of honing his craft, Frank partnered with Mike Wolfe, and together they launched American Pickers, which changed the landscape of reality television and antique collecting.

The Rise of American Pickers

American Pickers premiered on the History Channel on January 18, 2010. The show features Frank Fritz and Mike Wolfe traveling across the United States, visiting various locations to buy and sell antiques and collectibles. The premise of the show revolves around the thrill of the hunt, where they search for unique items that tell a story.

The show quickly gained popularity due to its entertaining format, dynamic hosts, and the fascinating world of antique collecting. American Pickers has become a cultural phenomenon, attracting millions of viewers and inspiring a new generation of collectors.
