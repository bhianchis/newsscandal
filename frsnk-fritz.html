Biography of Frank Fritz

Frank Fritz was born on October 11, 1965, in Davenport, Iowa. He grew up in a modest family environment, which instilled in him a strong work ethic and appreciation for the value of hard work. His passion for antiques began at a young age when he would accompany his father to flea markets and garage sales, sparking an interest that would define his future.
