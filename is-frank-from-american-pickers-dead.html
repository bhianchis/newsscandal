Frank Fritz Biography

Frank Fritz was born on October 11, 1965, in Davenport, Iowa. He grew up in a family that had a love for antiques, which played a significant role in shaping his career as a picker. Frank's journey to fame began with his friendship with Mike Wolfe, who shared his passion for collecting rare and vintage items. Together, they launched American Pickers, which became a massive hit on the History Channel.
