Biography of Jason Bateman

Jason Bateman was born on January 14, 1969, in Rye, New York. He is the son of Victoria and Kent Bateman, both of whom were involved in the entertainment industry. His father was a television director and his mother was a flight attendant. Bateman's early exposure to the industry played a significant role in shaping his career.
