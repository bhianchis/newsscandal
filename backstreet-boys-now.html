Biography of the Backstreet Boys

The Backstreet Boys were formed in 1993 in Orlando, Florida, by Lou Pearlman. The original lineup consists of AJ McLean, Howie Dorough, Nick Carter, Kevin Richardson, and Brian Littrell. They quickly rose to fame with their harmonious vocals and catchy pop tunes, becoming a staple in the music charts of the late 90s and early 2000s.
