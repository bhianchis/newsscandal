Biography of Cillian Murphy

Cillian Murphy was born on May 25, 1976, in Douglas, County Cork, Ireland. He grew up in a close-knit family, which greatly influenced his values and approach to life. Murphy attended University College Cork, where he studied Drama and Theatre Studies. His career began in the theater, where he quickly gained recognition for his talent and versatility.

Over the years, Murphy has starred in numerous acclaimed films and television series, including "28 Days Later," "Peaky Blinders," and "Inception." His ability to embody complex characters has earned him critical acclaim and a loyal fan base.
