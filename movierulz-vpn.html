How Does a VPN Work?

A VPN works by creating a secure tunnel between your device and the internet. When you connect to a VPN server, your internet traffic is routed through this server, which masks your IP address and encrypts your data. This process helps protect your online activities from hackers, ISPs, and other entities.

Encryption Techniques

VPNs use various encryption protocols to secure your data, including:

  • OpenVPN: A highly secure and open-source protocol.
  • IPSec: A suite of protocols that provides encryption and authentication.
  • L2TP: Often used in conjunction with IPSec for added security.

Types of VPNs

There are several types of VPNs available:

  • Remote Access VPN: Allows users to connect to a private network from a remote location.
  • Site-to-Site VPN: Connects multiple networks securely over the internet.
  • Mobile VPN: Designed for mobile devices, providing secure access on the go.
