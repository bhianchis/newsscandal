Biography of Chris Burke

Chris Burke was born on August 26, 1965, in Point Pleasant, New Jersey. He is best known for his role as Corky Thatcher on the ABC television series "Life Goes On," which aired from 1989 to 1993. Burke's portrayal of Corky, a teenager with Down syndrome, was groundbreaking and helped to bring visibility to individuals with disabilities in mainstream media.
