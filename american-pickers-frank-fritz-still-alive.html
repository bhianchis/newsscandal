Frank Fritz's Career on American Pickers

Frank Fritz gained national recognition as one of the co-hosts of "American Pickers," a show that premiered on the History Channel in 2010. Together with Mike Wolfe, Frank traveled across the United States, searching for hidden treasures in barns, attics, and flea markets. His expertise in antiques and charming personality quickly made him a fan favorite.

Throughout the show's run, Frank has unearthed remarkable collectibles, from vintage motorcycles to rare toys. His passion for picking and knowledge of history have contributed significantly to the show's success, attracting millions of viewers.
